Raiden

  • Setup markers at Raiden.
  • Ready check.
  • Kill Raiden.

Explanations

  • At some point during the fight, Raiden will disappear.
  • When this happens, we go to the 1 marker in the middle.
  • Raiden will reappear on either B or D, and he will then swing his sword towards one side of the arena.
  • Raiden will cleave the side the sword is pointing towards, so we need to go to the opposite side.
  • Before we move, we need to wait on the 1 marker for some puddles to appear.
  • As soon as the puddles appear, we move to the safe side.
