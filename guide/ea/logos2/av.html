  2. Check shells

  4. Do explanations.
  5. Do ready checks.
  6. Do pull timer (braveries, ranged/caster lb on 4)

Explanations

  • Color Change
    • AV changes his color throughout the fight.
    • He can be either light or dark.
    • His color changes how we handle certain mechanics.
  • Puddles
    • AV will create some puddles around the arena.
    • The puddles that match his color will expand in size.
    • If he is light, the light puddles will expand, if he is dark, the dark puddles will expand.
    • So for example, if AV is dark, we go close to light puddles.
    • Never stand inside the puddles, they will still do damage, but you can run across them while running to a safe spot.
  • Impact Stream
    • AV will divide the arena in two, one side dark and the other light.
    • We go to the side that is the opposite color of AV.
    • If AV is light, we go to dark, if AV is dark we go to light.
  • Turbulent Aether Orbs
    • AV will tether orbs to random players.
    • The orbs will slowly move towards the player and explode if they touch the player that they are tethered to.
    • Along with the orbs there will also be small puddles around the arena.
    • Both the orbs and the puddles will be either light or dark.
    • You must take your orb to a puddle of the opposite color to mitigate the damage.
    • If you cannot reach a puddle, just take your orb to the edge of the arena, so that you don't damage the group.
  • Javelin
    • Throughout the fight AV will do large cone attacks, so we always try to stay close to AV to dodge these.
