Art

  • Ready check.
  • Kill Art.

Explanations

  • Art switches between doing a donut AOE or a point blank AOE.
  • After the first two times, Art will summon 4 spears in the arena.
  • These 4 spears will copy the attacks that Art does.
  • Art will do an attack first, and then the spears will copy it.
  • If Art does a donut AOE, we need to move under Art, and then run to a spear.
  • If Art does a point blank AOE, we need to move away from Art, and then run under Art.
  • The attacks will be a lot faster once the spears are summoned, so sprint is very useful to dodge the attacks.
  • There are more mechanics in the fight, but Art is usually dead before we see them.
