Ozma

  • Remove tank marker.
  • Setup markers at Ozma.
  • /p we are on platform 1, we go to the blackhole buffer on A, we place meteors on 1 and A
  • Ready check.
  • Kill Ozma.

Host

  • Wait for cutscenes and ressing.
  • Tell party leaders to place markers.
  • Do ready checks for explanations.
  • Ask for bleed baiters.
Explanations
  • Meteors
    • Big Dark Purple AOE Marker - This is a big dark purple AOE marker that goes on players.
    • Wiggle - You can wiggle around to see if it's on you.
    • 2 per platform - There will be two meteors per platform.
    • Take to markers - The meteors need to be taken to the back and to the right. That is to the color coded markers on the arena.
    • Example - For example, if you are on the 1 platform, you will take the meteors to the red markers, which is 1 and A.
    • Adjust on the fly - You cannot know ahead of time who will get the meteors, so you have to look at where the other person is headed, and bring your meteor to the opposite marker.
  • Blackhole
    • Sucks out players - This is a mechanic that sucks out everyone who is not on a blackhole buffer.
    • Blackhole buffers = blue circles - The blackhole buffers are the blue circles that you can see around the arena.
    • Party leaders will show you - Your party leader will show you to your blackhole buffer later when we jump down.
  • Knockback
    • From buffer - There will be some knockbacks from the blue circles.
    • Front or back - We need to get knocked to the front or to the back. I will callout which direction.
    • Stand on the edge - When we do this, just stand on the front or back edge of the blue circle.
  • Acceleration Bombs
    • Debuff in Pyramid - This is a debuff that you can get in Pyramid shape.
    • Look in BA chat - If you look in BA chat, you can see what it looks like.
    • Explodes if doing anything - If you are doing anything when the debuff expires, it will explode and deal damage to you and everyone around you.
    • Untarget boss - To avoid this, untarget the boss and don't touch anything.
    • You can get Meteor too, focus on Meteor - It is possible to get both a Acceleration Bomb and a Meteor. If you do get both, focus on moving the meteor out, we can survive the Acceleration Bomb explosion.
    • Check debuff timer, move to ring - If you have enough time, you can move to the ring when you get an Acceleration Bomb. If you are on the ring, you won't get the Meteor.
  • Tanks
    • Magia = main tank - If you are in a magia party, you will be main tanking Ozma on your platform.
    • Logos = off tank - If you are in a logos party, you will be off tanking.
    • Main tank stance on - All main tanks turn your stance on now.
    • Off tank stance off - All off tanks turn your stance off now.
    • Tanks go lightning - All tanks please switch your element to lightning, which is the defensive element.
    • Magia auto off or defensive - And switch magia auto to defensive, or just disable it.
    • Ignore if you know - If you know what you are doing, you can just ignore this of course.
  • Off tank orbs
    • Cube phase - When cube phase starts, orbs will spawn on the ring.
    • 2 on the right - The off tanks need to run through the 2 orbs on the ring to the right.
    • Use mitigation - The orbs will explode and deal damage, so use mitigation.
    • No invuln - You cannot use invuln against the orb damage, it will not work.
    • Hug Ozma - The rest of us need to hug Ozma close, as the orbs might explode right behind us.
  • Main tanks
    • Stack in star - If it is star shape, just stay stacked with the group.
    • Left in cube - If it is cube shape, stay on the left side of the platform, as the auto attacks will be line AOE's.
  • Bleed baiting
    • Stay at back in pyramid - If you are bleed baiting, you just need to stand at the back during pyramid shape.
    • Move for meteor and shade - You only need to move for 2 things, the meteors and the Ozma shade. For the rest just stay at the back.
  • Follow callouts - There are a few more mechanics that I haven't explained, but it's mostly movement so I'll callout where to go, and you can just follow the callouts and the party.
After explanations

  • Once your party leader is happy, you can go back to your main platform.
  • Start 1 minute countdown.
  • You can hand out bravery if you have it.

  • Before you leave you might want to take the exit from the Arena, as there is some more story there.
