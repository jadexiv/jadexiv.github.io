Owain

  • Ready check.
  • Kill Owain.

Explanations

  • Owain will summon spears around the arena.
  • Owain will choose an element, and we need to go to a spear of the opposite element.
  • Owain can be either fire or ice.
  • If Owain is ice, we go to a fire spear.
  • If Owain is fire, we go to an ice spear.
  • There are more mechanics in the fight, but Owain is usually dead before we see them.
